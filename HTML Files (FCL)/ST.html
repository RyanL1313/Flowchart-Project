ST 281 - ELEMENTS OF STAT ANALYSIS

Semester Hours: 3

Descriptive statistics, fundamentals of probability theory, fundamentals of statistical inference, including estimation and hypothesis testing, and use of typical statistical package such as MINITAB. Prerequisites: MA 113 or MA 115 or Level 2 Placement.

ST 287 - APPLIED STATISTICS I

Semester Hours: 3

ST 487 - INTRO TO MATH STATISTICS

Semester Hours: 3

Brief review of basic probability theory, sampling distributions, estimations, hypothesis testing, experimental design, correlation and regression, analysis of variance, and nonparametric statistics. Prerequisites: MA 201 and either MA 385 or ISE 390.
