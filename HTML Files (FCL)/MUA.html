MUA 498 - SENIOR RECITAL

Semester Hour: 1.5

Represents the final semester of studio instruction on the primary instrument for all music majors except those pursuing the performance emphasis, who typically perform this recital as juniors. The recital must include a minimum of 30 minutes of music. The student must pass a recital jury at least two weeks before the scheduled recital performance.

MUA 499 - PERFORMANCE EMPHASIS RECITAL

Semester Hour: 1.5

For music majors pursuing the performance emphasis only. The Performance Emphasis Recital represents the final semester of studio instruction on the primary instrument for music majors pursuing the performance emphasis. The recital must include a minimum of 60 minutes of music. The student must pass a recital jury at least two weeks before the scheduled recital performance.
