WLC 409 - SR SEM: COMPARATIVE LANG & CUL

Semester Hours: 3

The senior seminar will explore a special topic relevant to all world languages and cultures; examples include but are not limited to education, family, fine arts and music, food, politics. Students will compare their own language and culture to other languages and cultures, develop connections to other languages and disciplines, and cultivate an understanding of and appreciation for world cultures and communities with the aim of understanding that there are multiple ways of viewing the world. This is the capstone course for majors who opt out of completing an international internship (WLC 410). Prerequisites: Two 300-level WLC courses and senior standing.
